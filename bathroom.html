Bathroom Installations

After kitchens, the bathroom can significantly increase the value and utility of your home. Experts agree that when selling your house, you can expect to recover more than the original cost of the bathroom installation. A luxurious room for relaxation and stress relief is a desirable requirement in a modern household, so every aspect of your bathroom must be well considered, from sanitary ware and fixtures to flooring and wall finishes. Most traditional bathroom layouts can be re-arranged to gain additional space for the "all-important shower" or simply renew the existing suite, with the option to increase the area by removing walls to unite a separate toilet and bathroom. When designing a new bathroom, ensure the floor is durable and 100% waterproof, capable of withstanding anticipated traffic for years; good quality tile floors, grout, and adhesive with plywood backing are recommended, along with non-gloss or non-slip tiles. Walls should also be waterproof, with fully tiled surfaces advised and bathroom water-repellent paint for ceilings. Ensure all joints are waterproof and finishing is of high quality. Choose good quality, branded bathroom furniture and fixtures that align with your desired aesthetic, and reserve enough space for a mirror with a shaver socket and lights; we recommend a top-quality, large mirror. Invest in a quality shower with adequate space, keeping drainage and servicing in mind. The toilet should be durable and fit the overall design, with electrical and plumbing lines well insulated to comply with Building Control and legal requirements. Center lighting to illuminate the room; we suggest IP66 light fittings that meet electrical regulations, and ensure proper ventilation with a power exhaust connected to the lighting circuit. If your windows are outdated, consider replacing them with double-glazed ones, and install a towel warmer for convenience. As bathrooms are typically the smallest rooms in the house, having sufficient storage units is essential to maintain a clean and uncluttered look. Bathroom renovations can be for various reasons, primarily for modernization, enhancing your home’s value and utility. Renovations can encompass layout changes, new fixtures, paint, and mirrors to give the bathroom a facelift, with inspiration found in magazines, show homes, or bathroom stores like Croydon Plumbers Merchants, Homebase, B&Q, and City Plumbing.
