House Extension

We at Southwest Builders can help you to make your dream House Extension.

Building an extension is a great way to create extra space. Whether it's to accommodate your growing family, or simply to help you spread out a little.

Extending the house is an economical way to provide for the extra space needed whilst maintaining all of the comforts of your existing environment and lifestyle. In addition to this, the investment in your current property will most definitely be reflected in its property value.

Important Factors to Consider

  • Will you require planning permission or building regulations?
  • Will you require structural calculations?
  • You are allowed to build under permitted development approximately 70 meters cubed, subject to no previous construction work.
  • Cost: £1300 - £1600 per square meter for a single-story extension. We offer no-obligation quotes.
  • Will the central heating boiler be capable of further expansion?
  • Will the builder install our kitchen or bath-suite?

Our Services

At Southwest Builders, we assist with planning, structural engineering, and obtaining necessary permissions. We work with both our Architect or your appointed Architect to ensure compliance with building regulations.

Steel Beams

For house extensions, steel beams are installed when required or as specified by an architect. This ensures the long-term stability of your home.

Velux Loft Windows

We recommend and install Velux windows for house extension roofs, offering the highest standards in design and performance.

Types of House Extensions

House extensions vary based on the shape, size, and design of your existing structure. With our help, we can transform your ideas into a versatile living space while adding value to your property.

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Prepare rough plans for the extension.
  2. Appoint an architect.
  3. Apply for planning permission and building regulations if necessary.
  4. Get estimates from multiple builders.
  5. Finalise plans and set a budget.
  6. Start the construction process: foundations, walls, roof, windows, electrics, plumbing, and finishing touches.
  7. Ensure you receive all appropriate certificates upon completion.

Planning the Build

Proper planning ensures a successful extension or loft conversion. Our team can guide you through the entire process, ensuring your project is completed efficiently and to the highest standard.
